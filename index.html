About Me


My name is Tyler Mayes. I am an Indy based junior web developer, Infantryman, and music teacher.
I have been in the Army for two years now, working as an Infantryman and an Infantry Team Leader. I have gained knowlegde in many aspects of leadership, discipline, and modern combat techniques in urban and jungle environments.
I have also worked as a percussion instructor both at Decatur Central and Perry Meridian, particularly as a drumline instructor. I teach 8th through 12th grade students almost daily and mentor them in rehearsal and private lessons.
My experience in retail and sales has given me job skills such as communication, marketing knowledge, advertising, and building bonds with customers and coworkers. I have only recently began to pursue web development as a career and I am passionate about design, user experience, and career advancement.
I have built and designed websites through Eleven Fifty Academy and freelance work, as you will see in my portfolio. You can learn more about my experience through LinkedIn or my resume.

If you have questions, please contact me!
